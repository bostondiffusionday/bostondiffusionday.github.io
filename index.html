About

Boston Diffusion Day is a community workshop focused on diffusion models and modern generative modeling, bringing together researchers in machine learning, statistics, neuroscience, vision, and adjacent fields. The day features a mix of invited and contributed talks, a poster session, and ample time for discussion. Our goals are to share recent results, highlight open problems, and build collaboration across Boston‑area groups.

  • Topics include: score‑based diffusion, theory & generalization, scaling laws, architectures (CNN/DiT/attention), evaluation, safety, and applications.
  • All levels welcome — from students to faculty and industry researchers.
  • Attendance is free; space may be limited. Registration required.
